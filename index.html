Mallory Johnson

| Geology | Hydrogeology | Cartography |



I don't love the idea of an artist statement, as I find myself drawn to multiple ideas at a time.

When I started making maps, I found myself inspired by urban environments and problems.

Recently, I find more inspiration in rural issues and geology, especially hydrogeology and anthropogenic pollution.

Today, I want to make maps to combine my inspirations of urban and rural problems; maps that have a clear message and dissuade disinformation.

(And, on occasion, some fancy fantasy maps.)
